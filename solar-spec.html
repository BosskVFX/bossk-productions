Installation Notes & Requirements
Electrical Requirements
⚡C-10 Licensed Electrician required — must hold valid CSLB C-10 license for all electrical work including inverter wiring, panel interconnection, and LADWP application.
⚡Rapid Shutdown built into EG4 18kPV — meets NEC 2020:690.12 and California Rule 21. No separate RSD device needed.
⚡AFCI built in — EG4 18kPV includes arc-fault protection per NEC 2020:690.11.
⚡LADWP NEM 2.0 interconnection — electrician to submit interconnection application to LADWP. System is below 20kW so no upgrade fees expected.
⚡PE-stamped plans required — single-line diagram and structural calculations to be signed by California licensed electrical engineer or C-10 licensee.
Mounting Requirements
🔩Roof type verification required — confirm roof material (asphalt, tile, etc.) before ordering specific IronRidge flashfoots. Tile roof requires different attachment hardware.
🔩24 attachment points minimum — one FlashFoot2 per panel, staggered to existing rafters. Rafter locate before drilling.
🔩Panel gap: 2" min, 10" max from underside of module to roof surface per LADBS code.
🔩No overhang — panels must not extend past ridge, hips, gable ends, or eaves.
🔩~600 sq ft roof area required — confirm available clear south/west-facing roof space via aerial view above before finalizing panel layout.
🔩Cable run from roof to inverter — owner will confirm inverter location (garage/utility room). DC run should stay under 50 ft where possible; use 10 AWG if over 30 ft.
