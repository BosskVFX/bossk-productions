Tigo TS4-A-O Power Optimizers — Optional Add-On
Why Optimizers
With a string inverter like the EG4 18kPV, one shaded panel drags down its entire string of 8. Tigo TS4-A-O optimizers make each panel independent so a shaded panel only hurts itself.

Since all panels are going on the clear right and front slopes, optimizers are optional insurance — not required. But if late-afternoon tree shadow ever creeps onto the string closest to the left slope, you'll want them on that string.
