Terms & Messaging Program

Last updated July 2026

This page describes the SMS text-messaging program operated by Bossk Productions LLC on behalf of the local business clients using our call-answering platform (including FrontDesk).

Program name

Bossk Productions / FrontDesk Missed-Call & Appointment Messaging

What this program does

When you call a business phone number that uses our platform and the call isn't answered, we send you a single text message inviting you to continue the conversation by text — so you can get booked or get an answer without having to call back. If you're a lead captured through the business's AI assistant, you may also receive a short follow-up message. These messages are triggered only by your own call to the business — we do not send unsolicited or recurring marketing texts.

Message frequency

Varies by user. Typically one message per missed call, plus any replies in that same conversation. This is not a recurring marketing program.

Message and data rates

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Opt-out and help

Reply STOP at any time to stop receiving messages from this number. Reply HELP for help, or contact us directly at team@bosskproductions.com or by text to 617-852-1005.
